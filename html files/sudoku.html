SUDOKU




VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted Sodoku on Wednesday, August 24, 2022, from 10:00 AM. This event included participants from our college as well as students from other colleges.

 

Sudoku is a Puzzle in which players insert the numbers one to nine into a grid consisting of nine squares. This helps the participant to improve their memory and thinking skills.



--> The event is restricted in time.



--> The team size is 1.




With a total of 509 participants, the event has been a great success.




About The Event


Sudoku is a puzzle in which players must place the numbers 1 to 9 into a grid made up of nine squares divided into a further nine smaller squares, ensuring that each number appears exactly once in each square, line, and vertical and horizontal space. The participant who finishes the task in the shortest amount of time wins.
