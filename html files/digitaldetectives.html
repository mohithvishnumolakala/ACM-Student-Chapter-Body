DIGITAL DETECTIVES



VVIT ACM Student Chapter hosted Digital Detectives on Friday, August 11th, 2023 from 10:00 AM. The event was part of the annual tech fest SPARDHA and was attended by students from various colleges.





➢ The event is restricted in time.



➢ The team size is 1.




The event received a great response from students with over 512 participants.





About The Event



The event revolves around identifying and rectifying potential errors within a web page, ultimately transforming the website's appearance to match the desired final output. Participants engage in a process of debugging and refining to ensure the website functions seamlessly and aligns with the specified design and functionality criteria.

 



In this challenge, participants are furnished with a web page containing a range of errors and challenges. Their task involves meticulously debugging the code and resolving these issues. Points are awarded based on the number of errors addressed and the complexity of the challenges overcome. The participant who successfully rectifies the highest number of errors, factoring in their difficulty, emerges as the victor of the competition.
