SURVIVAL SAGA




VVIT ACM Student Chapter organized Survival Saga on Friday, September 15th from 10:30 AM. The event was part of the AAVEGA 2023 and was attended by students from all years.



➢ The event is restricted in time.




➢ The team size is 3.




The event received a great response with over 450 participants.




About The Event



The event consists of three rounds in which the participants have to face different challenges to qualify for the following round. The events are focused on teamwork and increased communication among participants.

 

 

Round 1:



The initial round is about guessing the movie name based on the hints drawn on the board. Participants have to identify the relationship between the drawings and guess the movie name. They have to write their answers in the slips provided to them. the teams are displayed with a series of hints.

 

 


Round 2:



The next round is about guessing the product advertisements. The teams were displayed with a video of an advertisement and they had to guess the product based on the videos. They will be displayed with a series of videos and have to note down the answers.

 


 


Round 3:




The final round consisted of three games being played by three members of the team. One player needs to do a bottle flip challenge, the second player needs to solve a Rubik’s cube and the last player needs to solve a maze. The team that manages to solve all three games in the shortest time will be the winner.
