CRACK THE WORD


Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology(VVIT), under VVIT Guntur ACM-W Student Chapter conducted the event “Crack The Word” on 7th February 2020 at 2:30PM in II IT A(507) New Block.

CRACK THE WORD is a two level event which enables students to know how good they are with the fast updating technology and letting them explore and test themselves how familiar they are with the technical terms.


  • The event is open for CSE & IT departments.
  • The team size is 2.

The participation and response to the event was great from both 1st and 2nd year students of CSE & IT departments accounting to total of 40 teams.


Round 1:

In the first round, a word that fits the given blank spaces had to be found using the given hints. Total of 20 questions were displayed and each of them had to be answered within one minute. The teams that cracked highest number of words were qualified to the next round. Students showed up great interest in taking part and gave a tough competition. A total of 13 teams were qualified to the second round.
