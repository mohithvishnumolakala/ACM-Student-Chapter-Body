BLUR IMAGES




VVIT ACM Student Chapter hosted Blur Images on Thursday, August 10th, 2023 from 10:00 AM. The event was part of the annual tech fest Spardha and was attended by students from various institutions.

 

The event received a great response with over 600 participants.

 




About The Event



Blur Images" is an intriguing individual event that challenges participants' keen observation and knowledge. In this captivating competition, participants are presented with a collection of images featuring prominent figures from the realms of politics, technology, movies, and beyond. However, there's a twist – the images are deliberately blurred, making identification a fascinating puzzle.

 

 


As participants immerse themselves in this captivating challenge, they engage in a stimulating exercise of memory, recognition, and association. The event not only offers a platform for showcasing knowledge but also fosters a sense of curiosity and exploration, encouraging participants to dive into various domains to uncover what is behind each image.
