TRIO VERSE




VVIT ACM Student Chapter hosted Trio Verse on Friday, August 11th, 2023 from 10:00 AM. The event was part of the annual tech fest Spardha and was attended by students from various institutions.

The event received a great response from students with over 700 Participants.

The event consists of 3 various levels.

 

About The Event



 

Alpha Hunt:


"Alpha Hunt" is an intriguing alphabet-based game that challenges participants' word skills. A collection of paper slips, each bearing a lengthy word, is placed within a box. The slips are accompanied by paper cups, each marked with an individual alphabet, forming a heap. Participants randomly choose two word slips and must then identify the paper cups bearing letters present in the chosen words. Unused cups form another heap. This arrangement requires strategic cup selection and swift decision-making, ensuring cups are drawn within the designated time frame.




Tongue Tornado:


"Tongue Tornado" centers around mastering tongue twisters. Participants draw two slips, each containing a challenging tongue twister. Within a minute, participants familiarize themselves with the twister's pronunciation. They must then attempt to repeat the twister as rapidly as possible, achieving a specified repetition count within the given time limit. This level celebrates diction and articulation in an exhilarating linguistic challenge.



Twin Tracker:


"Twin Tracker" introduces a pairs-finding game. Participants are presented with a box containing pairs of similar objects. In the first turn, a participant selects an object at random. In the second turn, they must locate and pick the matching object for the one chosen in the first turn. A time limit and a predetermined number of correct pairs must be achieved to secure victory. This level showcases memory and quick thinking in a dynamic matching pursuit.The event provides dual arrangements for each game, accommodating a large crowd. With two participants engaging simultaneously, "Trioverse" offers a platform for diverse skills and entertainment, making it a standout experience within the event.
