ACM TECHNICAL EVENTS FOR VIVA VVIT



Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology, every year organizes a technical fest VIVA VVIT which provides a platform to the students of various engineering colleges to participate and showcase their talent and also to strengthen their resumes.


For the academic year 2017-18, VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter took a lead in organizing the following technical events for the maha fest VIVA VVIT 2K17-18 ASHRAJAN on 21, 22 of December 2017.


With a lot of enthusiasm and determination, the Chapter members and faculty coordinator of VVIT Guntur ACM student chapter organized and volunteered the events. The chair of VVIT Guntur ACM student chapter Ajay k helped in organizing these events with fun.



  • Potential Professor
  • Codejan
  • Query Cracking
  • Design Mania
  • Debugging


POTENTIAL PROFESSOR



This event gives the participants an opportunity to showcase their presentation skills by presenting on a topic to the panel of judges. It strives to explore the inquisitiveness of young minds by giving their incredulous ideas a definitive direction.The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.Registrations are done at online where the registration process was placed at the website vivavvit.com and spot registrations are also available before the event starts.


The participants are allowed to register in the potential professor event through the website and they had submitted their abstracts of their respective topics with title and subject before the two days of the event.


The officers of the ACM VVIT GUNTUR Student Chapter shortlisted the students who are registered through mails based on the presentation of their abstracts. The shortlisted participants are intimated through mails and promoted to present their respective topics.


RULES AND REGULATIONS:


  • Technical related topics must be chosen(Innovative topics are preferred)
  • Only two members per team are allowed.
  • Usage of power point presentation is not mandatory.
  • The maximum time given to each team is 7minutes.
  • Only participants who participate the preliminary abstract submission round were eligible for the final presentation.
  • Presentation will last for 10minutes.
  • Presentation skills, body language and other parameters are judged.


The faculty coordinators of this potential professor is M. Prashanth judged the students who presented their ideas. And the supporting faculty also joined the faculty coordinator for the judgment.




CSE HOD honouring the winner by presinting the prize


After the presentations of all the students, the faculty coordinators take their time and finalize the two students from the participants and announced the results.


The students are awarded and certified with their performance and all the participants have gained their participation certificates issued by VVIT, Guntur and ACM VVIT Guntur Student Chapter.





           CODEJAN



CodeJan is an excellent opportunity to unlock your logical ability and coding skills. Registrations are done at online where the registration process was placed at the website vivavvit.com and spot registrations are also available before the event starts.


RULES AND REGULATIONS:


  • A screening test was conducted in span of 20minutes.
  • Screening test is a team consisting of 2 members per team. Selected teams were promoted to the final round. 
  • Final round was an individual competition consists of 3-4 complex programs which the participants should solve in span of 2hours of time..
  • Participants can use any one of the following programming languages provided C, CPP, JAVA, PYTHON. 
  • Complexity of code is also considered in judgment.
  • The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.

Students from various colleges attended to take the test in solving the complex problem using programming languages.


The screening test was conducted to shortlist the participants for coding. The screening test was conducted with team of two members includes technical related questions for a span of 20minutes.


ACM members facilitated the participants by distributing the question papers to the participants for the screening test.This event focuses on testing the coding skills and ability of the students to understand the code and interpret it according to the requirements.Student’s participation from various colleges for the event CodeJan is 60 members. They formed into teams of two members each.The participants in screening test are intimated to report after one hour for the result who are promoted to the round-2 that is coding.


The faculty coordinators, officers and members of ACM evaluated the screening test papers and shortlisted the members to the final round to solve the complex problems based on the highest mark. 40 members are promoted to final round to code the problem using programming languages.


The members were report to the computer lab to solve the complex problem using programming languages C, CPP, JAVA and PYTHON with in the span of 2 hours.The students are given with 2 hours of time to solve the problem using programming languages. The faculty coordinators checked the outputs of their problems.


After the completion of two hours, they checked all the outputs and took hal and hour to judge the students. After the participation of all the students, the faculty coordinators take their time and finalize the two students from the participants and announced the results.



CSE HOD honouring the winner by presinting the prize


The students are awarded and certified with their performance and all the participants have gained their participation certificates issued by VVIT, Guntur and ACM VVIT Guntur Student Chapter.




QUERY CRACKING


 


Query cracking is a challenging task for all CSE/IT/MCA students which test the knowledge of building a query, breaking a complex query into a simple one, etc.


Registrations are done at online where the registration process was placed at the website vivavvit.com and spot registrations are also available before the event starts.


RULES AND REGULATIONS:


  • This is an individual participation event where the participants solve the few complex queries related to database.
  • We prefer the usage of MySql.
  • Participants provided the database and the queries that are needed to be solved.
  • Queries with minimum complexity were given the preference.
  • The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.


The students are allowed to take the lab and are allowed to solve the queries and simplify the complex queries.


After the participation of all the students, the faculty coordinators and judges have examined and finalized two students from the participants as winners. The judges have announced the winners and congratulated them for their performance.



CSE HOD honouring the winner by presinting the prize


The students are awarded and were given with certificates for their performance..The winners were given the certificates and a shield as a token of appreciation by VVIT, Guntur and VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter.




DESIGN MANIA


This event is designed for those students who are interested in designing. The apps may be mobile applications or the web pages.The participants were given with a theme and they have to design a webpage or a mobile application according to that. The participants are allowed to take part in teams of two.


THEMES: (any one)

  • E-Commerce
  • Job portal

Rules and Regulations:


  • No usage of CMS.
  • Web app/mobile app should be developed from scratch.
  • Marks are given based on the UI design and primary functionality.
  • A team event with two members.
  • Systems were provided.
  • Partial code is allowed through pen drive only.



The theme was decided by the judges. All the teams were provided with a computer. They were given with sufficient amount of time.


The teams are scored based on the user interface of the app they came up with. The more easy and simple the interface is the higher they will be scored.


The winners were given the shields and winning certificate.


The members of VVIT Guntur ACM student chapter organized this event with a lot of zeal.
