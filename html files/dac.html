DIVIDE AND CONQUER


VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted Divide and conqueron Monday, August 22, 2022, from 11:30 AM. This event included participants from our college as well as students from other colleges.



➢ The event is restricted in time.



➢ The team size is 1.



➢ It is a two- level event.


With a total of 211 participants, the event has been a great success.






About The Event



A word-guessing contest is called Divide and Conquer. Participants will see pictures that combine to become words in this activity. This will enable the participants to gauge their level of understanding and competency in the field.



Round 1:



Round 1 is done for the participants in the morning session. There are a few slides that are composed of word-like visuals. The words for each slide should be identified by the participants. There are a total of 15 slides given, with a 15-second presentation time for each slide. The participants that scored the highest are advance to the next round



 



Round 2:



In the afternoon session round-2 is been held for the participants. A total of 24 participants are selected for this round. The words for each slide should be identified by the participants. There are a total of 20 slides given, with a 30-second presentation time for each slide. The contestant who received the highest score is declared the winner.



 


The team which reaches the final destination on time will be declared as winner.
