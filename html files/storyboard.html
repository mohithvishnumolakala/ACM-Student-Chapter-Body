STORYBOARD



VVIT Guntur ACM Student Chapter hosted Storyboard on Wednesday, August 24, 2022, from 1:00 PM. This event included participants from our college as well as students from other colleges.



Story board is mainly about the visualization. Here the participants are divided into teams. The participants are given with a system along with the internet facility. Using them, they narrated a story depicting the theme given to them.



--> The event is restricted in time.




-->The team size is 2.




With a total of 276 participants, the event has been a great success.




About The Event



Story Board is an innovative and creative event in which some sets of themes will be displayed. Participants can choose their theme based on their interests. Participants should represent the selected theme in pictorial form within a time limit of 1 hour 15 minutes. The presentation may be a PowerPoint presentation, Canvas, or Comics.






The HackerEarth platform was used to organize the event, each participant used a separate HackerEarth account. participants are provided with questions from different sets.



The weightage of marks is based on the complexity of the problem. The participant who manages to secure the most marks will be declared the winner.
